Our Story

Started Small, Built on Trust

ProMove was born in 2019 from a simple frustration: moving services in Malaysia were either unreliable, overpriced, or just plain careless with people's belongings.

Our founders — three friends with backgrounds in logistics, construction, and home services — decided to do things differently. The result? A tight-knit 3-person team that treats every job like it's their own home on the line.

Today, we've completed over 500 jobs across Klang Valley, built a reputation on honesty and precision, and grown entirely through word-of-mouth from satisfied customers.
