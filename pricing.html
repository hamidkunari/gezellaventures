Pricing FAQ

Common Pricing Questions

Straight answers to the questions we get asked most about our rates and payment.

💬 Ask About Pricing
Are the prices fixed or can I negotiate?
+

Our starting rates are guidelines. Final pricing depends on property size, service scope, and access difficulty. We provide a confirmed fixed quote before any work begins — no on-site "surprises".

Do prices include materials and supplies?
+

It depends on the service. For cleaning, we bring our own supplies. For painting, paint cost is typically quoted separately based on your colour and area. We'll always specify what's included in your quote.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept cash, bank transfer (TnG / MayBank / CIMB / RHB), and DuitNow QR. A deposit may be required for large bookings, with the balance payable on completion.

Is there a travel or transport fee?
+

No travel fee within Klang Valley. For jobs in Seremban, Selangor outskirts, or other states, a small travel surcharge may apply — which we'll communicate clearly in your quote.

Can I get a discount for bundled services?
+

Yes! Booking multiple services together typically results in a better overall rate compared to booking each separately. Our Standard and Premium packages are designed exactly for this.
